Kakoli Bose is a self-taught Mandala artist who discovered her passion after transitioning from oil painting. Inspired by Carl Jung's insights on the therapeutic power of mandalas, Kakoli creates intricate designs that provide a sacred space for self-connection and tranquility. Her talent has earned her recognition, including winning the Instagram Mandala Challenge in 2021 and 2022. Explore her captivating portfolio on Instagram @kbpencil.
