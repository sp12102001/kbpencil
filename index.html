KBPencil by Kakoli Bose

Kakoli Bose, a self-taught Mandala artist, evolved from traditional oil painting to creating therapeutic Mandala designs inspired by Carl Jung. Her work promotes tranquility and self-connection.

Acknowledged for her distinct style, Kakoli has won the Instagram Mandala Challenge in 2021 and 2022. Explore her art that not only captivates but also inspires healing.
