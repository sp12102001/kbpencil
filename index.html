Kakoli Bose Artistry

Kakoli Bose is a self-taught Mandala artist whose work transitions from traditional oil painting to intricate Mandala designs. Inspired by Carl Jung's theories on the therapeutic power of mandalas, Kakoli's creations are crafted to foster self-connection and tranquility.

Her unique approach and dedication have led her to win accolades such as the Instagram Mandala Challenge in 2021 and 2022. Dive into the world of her captivating mandalas and experience art that heals and inspires.
