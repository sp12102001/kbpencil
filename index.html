My artistic journey began with oil painting, but for the past 6 years, I've dedicated myself to the mesmerizing world of Mandala art. Working primarily on commissioned pieces, I create intricate, detailed designs inspired by nature, incorporating floral and organic patterns. My specialty lies in hand-drawn mandalas with multimedia elements, emphasizing fine detailing that captures both the eye and the spirit.

  • Winner of Instagram Mandala Challenge (2021, 2022)
  • Judge for Art Competition by Emirates Environmental Group, Dubai
  • Exhibitor at World Art Dubai 2024, with confirmed participation for 2025
  • Successfully completed over 15 commissioned art pieces
